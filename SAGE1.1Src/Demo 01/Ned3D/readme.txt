Keyboard commands for this demo are:

  Esc   - Quit
  ~     - Toggle show/hide the console
            Type 'help -a' (no quotes) when the console is visible to get a
            list of commands supported by the console.
  
  Mouse - Rotates the camera (left/right and tilt up/tilt down)
  Up    - Moves the camera forward
  Down  - Moves the camera backward
  Left  - Strafes the camera left
  Right - Strafes the camera right
